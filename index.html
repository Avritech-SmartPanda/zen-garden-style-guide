Theme

The theme of our CSS Zen Garden will be something minimalistic. We have some great ideas on how to present a webpage as well as how some of the elements on the page should be displayed.

Our typeface should be a cursive for the headings and a sans serif for the body. We have chosen to use the Open Sans (sans serif) and Rancho (cursive) fonts throughout this project.

Typography

We have chosen the open sans font as it is elegant and sophisticated, yet modern. It is beautifully legible, offers an excellent reading experience, and has a superior typographic range that includes accents, non-Latin characters, and more.
